How to Add Your Profile Image

Step 1: Prepare Your Image

  1. Use a professional headshot or profile photo
  2. Recommended size: 600x600 pixels (square)
  3. Format: JPG or PNG
  4. File size: Keep under 2MB for fast loading

Step 2: Save Your Image

  1. Rename your image to: profile.jpg
  2. Save it in the folder: public/images/
  3. The full path should be: public/images/profile.jpg

Step 3: Refresh Your Website

  1. After adding the image, refresh your browser
  2. Your image will appear in both the Hero and About sections
  3. It will have beautiful 3D effects and animations!

Alternative File Names

If you prefer a different filename, you can also use:

  • profile.png
  • headshot.jpg
  • avatar.jpg

Note: If you use a different filename, you'll need to update the image paths in the code.
